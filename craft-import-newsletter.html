SUPPLIER SPOTLIGHT: SUN KING BREWERY
Indianapolis-based Sun King Brewery will be distributing their beers outside of Indiana for this first time ever beginning Monday, August 21. Founded in 2009 by Clay Robinson and Dave Colt, Sun King is now the second-largest brewery in Indiana, producing about 35,000 bbls of beer in 2016.

They are well-known for their Sunlight Cream Ale, a GABF gold medal winning cream ale, along with their line of IPAs, barrel-aged beers, and sours.

Goose Island Juicy Douple IPA
 
Juicy Double is a double IPA brewed with orange juice and generously hopped with seven hop varieties. 8.3% ABV, 70 IBU. Orange juice is added to the wort and then fermented out during normal fermentation to give subtle orange citrus flavor.


Available formats: 4/6 LN, 1/6 BBLs
STR: 7/15/17
More Information
Goose Island Imperial 49 IPA
 
Imperial 49 is an imperial IPA brewed with 5 hop varieties: Amarillo, Chinook, Cascade, Calypso, & Galaxy which gives intense tropical and pine aromas with bold and complex hop flavor. 8.4% ABV, 80 IBU. 

Available formats: 4/6 LN, 1/6 BBLs
STR: 7/15/17

Founders Green Zebra
