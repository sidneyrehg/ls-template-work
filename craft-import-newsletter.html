NEW PRODUCTS
Sun King Sunlight Cream Ale
 

Sunlight Cream Ale celebrates American brewing tradition by balancing smooth malt complexity with a crisp, clean finish.

Sunlight was the winner of the Gold Medal in the Golden or Blonde Ale category at the 2015 Great American Beer Festival.

Available formats: 4/6 12oz CN 1/2BBL
STR: 8/21/17
More Information
Sun King Wee Mac
 

Wee Mac Scottish-style Ale is an enticing and malty brown ale with caramel, cocoa, and hazelnut character balanced with toffee undertones.

Wee Mac was the Gold Medal winner at the 2010 Indiana Brewers' Cup in the English Brown Ale category.

Available formats: 4/6 12oz CN 1/2BBL
STR: 8/21/17
